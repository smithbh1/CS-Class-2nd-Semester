Santaquin: Home of the Orchard Days

Originally founded in 1851, Santaquin was originally named "Summit City" and was settled by pioneers. It was renamed to "Santaquin" in 1856 in honor of a friendy, local native chieftan. Santaquin is also know for its famous "Orchard Days", a celebration of local farmers and fruit-growers. They celebrate in the ofr of parades, rodeos, carnivals, scavenger hunts, and much more!


As of 2019 there was an estimate 12,865 residents with ~42% of those residents being under the age of 18. The median annual income per household was $45,323 and the per capita income was $13,725.
